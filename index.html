Flow helps you to develop your skillset, seamlessly, every day.

Flow is

Flexible

In your pocket or on your desktop, Flow adapts your feed whether you have 2 minutes or 1 hour, at home or commuting, whether you are tired or excited

Personalized

Our AI learns how you learn. It adapt contents topics, difficulty, format, tones, etc... to your learning profile, so you always feel engaged.

Efficient

We use recognized neuroscience retention techniques, and provide you consolidation tools to help you learn for life.

Social

Your friends are on Flow. As well as collegues, enthusiasts and experts of the fields you explore. They recommend you great content, and keep you motivated.

Collaborative

Individuals, experts or companies can build and offer learning paths for the community, for free or Premium

Multi-Format

Various type of content: video, article, podcast, news, mooc, question/answer, mooc... to fit to your preferences and keep you engaged

How we do that

1. Gather Content

Our robots gather all types of content from all over internet. Videos, Podcast, Articles, Newspapers, Q&A, Mindmaps, Quizzes, Games...

2. Filter & Annotate

We filter and keep the BEST content serving your progression. We attach a bunch of metadata, helpfull to capture how your brain naturally learns.

3. Classify & Organize

Our AI, relying on experts insights, classifies and organized all content in our knowledge graph, to create personalized and coherent learning paths.

4. Feed & Capture

We insert relevant content in your feed, detect your preferences and capture how you learn best. Using neuroscience principles, we continuously improve your engagement and retention
